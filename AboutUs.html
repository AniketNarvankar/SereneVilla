Serene's Nature Villa

Serene's Nature Villa is nestled in the scenic Ratnagiri district, approximately 26 km from Dapoli and just 10 km from Dabhol. It's an ideal weekend getaway for travelers from Mumbai or Pune, perfect for a relaxing 3-day trip surrounded by nature's beauty. We offer comfortable stay options along with delicious Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner. Guests can explore nearby attractions during the day and return to unwind at the villa in the evening. For trip planning tips and a list of must-visit places in the area, click on Places to Visit.
