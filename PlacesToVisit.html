Plan a 3-Day Trip to Serene's Nature Villa:

Day 1: Start from Mumbai or Pune around 7:00 AM and reach Serene's Nature Villa by 12:30 PM. Enjoy a relaxing lunch, then head out at 3:30 PM to explore Asud Keshavraj Mandir, Kade Varcha Ganpati in Anjarle, and Murud Beach. Return to the villa at night for a peaceful stay and dinner.

Day 2: After breakfast, begin your day at 8:00 AM with visits to Guhagar Beach and Hedvi Ganesh Mandir. Return by 2:00 PM for lunch. In the evening, around 5:00 PM, visit Chandika Devi Mandir in Dabhol, Bhairi Bhavani Mandir, and Dabhol Beach. Return to the villa for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: Enjoy breakfast in the morning, followed by a visit to Koleshwar Mandir. After returning and having lunch, you can begin your journey back to Mumbai or Pune around 3:30 PM.
